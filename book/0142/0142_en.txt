In-vitro experiments carried out, it was possible to show that phenylalkylamines themselves function as active ingredients (see e.g. Chapter 2, 8.4.1.2 and 8.5.1). Substance 47 also bears the name P2P (phenyl-2-propanone) and is a precursor for the synthesis of amphetamine or methamphetamine.


Summary

    The most prominent representative of the phenethylamines with a heteroatom on the side chain is ephedrine with its nor- and pseudo-isomers. Ephedrine shows its stimulating effects primarily through interaction with noradrenergic neurotransmission. Some beta-keto derivatives such as methcathinone (13) have been shown to be powerful stimulants. Strongly electron-withdrawing side-chain substituents such as halogens, nitriles or CF3 groups drastically reduce the basicity of the respective amines, so that protonation of nitrogen is much poorer and interaction with biological targets in protonated form is very difficult under physiological conditions.
